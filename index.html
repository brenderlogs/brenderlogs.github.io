Who Am I?

I'm a mathematician / pedagogue, living and working in Montreal. I also like to spend time brewing. You can contact me at myfirstname.mylastname@gmail.com.

Mathematics

As a student, my specialization was mathematical logic and category theory, though I've also studied formal languages, computability & complexity, and cryptography.

Nowadays, I like to pick a good text in an area I don't know much about and spend a while learning something new. I'm currently into elliptic curves, bayesian statistics, and python.
